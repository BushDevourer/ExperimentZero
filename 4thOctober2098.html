You reach a house.


You enter into a small hallway.
There's a small crate to your left,
and a dead cat to your right.

At the back of the house,
there is a small garden with a crooked wicker fence.
A shed resides in the far left corner.

The shed is locked. Must need a key.

Don't eat cats. Seriously.

You pick up the crate. Inside,
there are 3 chewed up lego bricks,
and an unusually heavy can of soup.

You continue into the hall.
There are 3 doors on on either side.

You open door 1.
Inside, there's a small TV set on a wooden table.
The screen flickers with a strange light.

You open door 2.

In the can, there's some damp sand.

You empty the sand onto the floor.
A key presents itself.
